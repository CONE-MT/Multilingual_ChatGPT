Dialogue

The recognized text will be displayed in the input box below, and you can also manually adjust or enter new text in the input box.

In the right sidebar, you can click on "ChatGPT Dialogue" to directly start a conversation with ChatGPT.

In the left sidebar, you can choose to translate your input into your desired language before submitting it to ChatGPT. You can either click on "Translate (CONE)" to use our provided multilingual translation service or click on "Translate (ChatGPT)" to obtain translation results from ChatGPT. The translated content will appear in the "translation text" section. You can also adjust the translation results yourself, and then click "ChatGPT Dialogue" to start a conversation with ChatGPT. The content returned by ChatGPT will appear in the "ChatGPT response text" section. You can also choose to use translation services to translate the ChatGPT response into your input language, and the "translation of ChatGPT response text" will display the corresponding translation.
